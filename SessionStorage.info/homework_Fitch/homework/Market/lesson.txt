POST - orqali biz yangi bitta obyekt 'Post' yaratish mumkin.
Agar yangi post yaratmoqchi bolsak misol uchun shu tartibda qoshsek boladi

fetch("https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com/posts",{
    method:'POST',-----                                   //post metodi
    headers:{"Content-Type":"application/json"},
    body:JSON.stringify({
        UserId:1,
        title:'j-t Nazaryasi',
        body:"nimadir",

    })

});
.then((data)=>console.log(data)),
.catch((data)=>console.log(data))


PUT - orqali biz API dagi malumotlarni ozgartira olamiz va uni "PUT" orqali bajaramiz shu misol kabi 

fetch"https://jsonplaceholder.typicode.com/posts",
    method:'PUT',-----                                   //PUT metodi
    headers:{"Content-Type":"application/json"},
    body:JSON.stringify({
        UserId:1,
        title:'j-t Nazaryasi',  //Malum bir Id dagi malumot ozgaradi
        body:"nimadir",

    })


DELETE - Serverda mavjud resursni o'chirish uchun ishlatiladi. 
Masalan, foydalanuvchi hisobini yoki maqolani o'chirishda.
